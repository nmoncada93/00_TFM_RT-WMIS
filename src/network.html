Real Time Global Observation Network

The Real Time Global Observation Network is based on GNSS observation data collected via broadcasters from IGS, AUSCORS, EUREF, IGN and RBMC networks. The sampling rate is 1hz and for a ser of 47 currently available stations (including 3 collocated pairs) mostly located in regions prone to scillation activity.
